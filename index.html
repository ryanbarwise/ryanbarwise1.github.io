About Me

My name is Ryan, and I am originally from El Paso, TX. I have been lucky enough to have spent most of my profesional career as a trumpet player. I have held full time orchestral positions in New York, Virginia, and Alabama and for the past four years was located in Iowa where I taught trumpet lessons at the University of Northern Iowa. Considerable amounts of my time in Iowa was also spent driving around and braving the Midwestern elements, including a polar vortex, so that I could continue playing with various orchestras. It was an experience, but I am excited to be in Florida where I am currently enrolled in the UCF Coding Bootcamp and excited for new life opportunities!
