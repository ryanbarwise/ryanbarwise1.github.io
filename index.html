About Me

My name is Ryan, and I am originally from El Paso, TX. I have been lucky enough to have spent most of my profesional career as a trumpet player. I have held full time orchestral positions in New York, Virginia, and Alabama. For the past four years I was located in Iowa where I spent my time teaching trumpet lessons including some time as an instructor at the University of Northern Iowa. Considerable amounts of my time in Iowa was also spent driving and braving the Midwestern elements, including a polar vortex, so that I could continue playing with various orchestras. It was an experience, but I am excited to be in Florida where I am currently enrolled in the UCF Coding Bootcamp and excited for new life opportunities!
